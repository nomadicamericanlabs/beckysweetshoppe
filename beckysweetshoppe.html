About me

I am passionate when it comes to making sweets.

I have been making since I was a little girl in Texas. I have served my country as Child Development Specialist for over 20 years in direct support of the United States Air Force, Army and Navy. I have a Associates of Science Degree in Nursing from Angelo State University. My highest level of honor and joy comes from serving others and an honest day's work in what capacity I serve in. I possess a strong sense small business acumen, I received and academic certificate of achievement in Business Plan writing through Brookdale Community College.
